Target Sectors

The fund focuses on infrastructure projects with high potential for technological integration in the following key sectors:

Energy

Renewable energy projects (solar, wind), smart grid technology, and power generation and distribution.

Water

Water treatment and sanitation systems, and smart water management technologies.

Gas

Natural gas infrastructure, pipelines, and distribution networks.

Logistics

Supply chain infrastructure, smart ports, and tech-enabled transportation networks.

Agriculture

Agri-tech infrastructure, smart farming, and food logistics.
