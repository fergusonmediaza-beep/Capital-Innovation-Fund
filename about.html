About Us

The Capital Innovation Fund brings together technology and capital to unlock Africa’s next wave of infrastructure growth. Our digital platform tracks projects end-to-end using IoT sensors, geospatial data, drones, and AI analytics.

By turning traditionally opaque infrastructure into transparent, investable assets, we help accelerate delivery across Energy, Water, Gas, Logistics, and Agriculture sectors.

Mission

To drive sustainable and transparent infrastructure development through technology, creating long-term value for investors and African communities.

Vision

To be the leading catalyst for Africa’s digital infrastructure transformation, ensuring a more efficient, resilient, and transparent future.

Core Values

Our success is measured by the positive impact our projects create across Africa.

Innovation

We embrace technology that drives meaningful change.

Transparency

We provide full visibility to build trust and accountability.

Sustainability

We design long-term economic and environmental impact.

Partnership

We build strong collaborative relationships across Africa.
