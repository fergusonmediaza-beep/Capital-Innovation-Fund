Our Solutions

The Capital Innovation Fund brings together technology and capital to unlock Africa’s next wave of infrastructure growth. We provide an integrated digital platform that tracks projects end-to-end, utilising IoT sensors, geospatial data, drones, and AI analytics, providing funders with verified, real-time insights from construction to operation. By turning traditionally opaque projects into transparent, investable assets we accelerate funding and improve delivery across Energy, Water, Gas, Logistics, and Agriculture sectors.
